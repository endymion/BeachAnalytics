This chart shows a range of estimates for the number of SARS-CoV-2 infections, based on the number of confirmed COVID-19 cases.

The range shown in the chart is from a study estimating the number of infections relative to the number of reported cases from the Centre for Mathematical Modelling of Infectious Diseases at the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine.

The estimated ranges for various countries are updated periodically, and I'm using the estimated range for the United States. I update my charts whenever the estimated range is updated.

Sources

The confirmed case count data comes from the daily updates from the Florida Division of Emergency Management.
