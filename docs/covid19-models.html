    COVID-19 Forecasting Models

    This is the first in a series of features about the COVID-19 computer forecast models. These features will explore the background, strengths, weaknesses, and impact of each model.

    Imperial College of London COVID-19 Forecasting Model

    Arguably the most influential computer forecasting model in history, this model is the one that led the UK and US governments to begin mitigation measures.
